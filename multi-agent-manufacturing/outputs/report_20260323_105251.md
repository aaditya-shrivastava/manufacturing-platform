## Risk Assessment
The main risks associated with the selected suppliers are:
* Supply chain risks: dependence on a single supplier, potential material shortages, and logistics disruptions.
* Single-source risk: relying on a single supplier for critical components, which can lead to supply chain disruptions if the supplier experiences any issues.
* Quality risks: potential defects or non-conformities in the supplied parts, which can impact the overall quality of the final product.
To mitigate these risks, it is recommended to:
* Diversify the supplier base to reduce dependence on a single supplier.
* Develop a comprehensive supply chain risk management plan to identify and mitigate potential risks.
* Implement a quality control process to ensure that the supplied parts meet the required standards.
