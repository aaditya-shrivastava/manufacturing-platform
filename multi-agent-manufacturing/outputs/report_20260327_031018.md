## Risk Assessment
The main risks associated with these suppliers include:
* Supply chain risks: Disruptions in the supply chain can lead to delays in delivery and increased costs.
* Single-source risk: Relying on a single supplier can lead to vulnerabilities in the event of supplier insolvency or quality issues.
* Quality risks: Failure to meet quality certifications, such as ISO 9001:2015 and RoHS compliance, can result in defective components and project delays.
To mitigate these risks, it is essential to:
* Diversify the supplier base to reduce reliance on a single supplier.
* Conduct regular audits to ensure compliance with quality certifications.
* Develop a contingency plan to address potential supply chain disruptions.
