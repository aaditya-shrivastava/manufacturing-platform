## Risk Assessment
The main risks associated with sourcing carbon fiber sheets from these suppliers include:
- **Supply Chain Risks:** Disruptions in raw material supply, production capacity constraints, and logistics issues may impact lead times and product availability.
- **Single-Source Risk:** Relying on a single supplier may pose risks if the supplier experiences production issues, quality control problems, or financial difficulties.
- **Quality Control Risks:** Ensuring consistent product quality is crucial, and any deviations may impact the performance and safety of aerospace applications.
- **Regulatory Risks:** Changes in regulatory requirements, such as updates to AS9100D or NADCAP, may require suppliers to adapt their processes and certifications.
