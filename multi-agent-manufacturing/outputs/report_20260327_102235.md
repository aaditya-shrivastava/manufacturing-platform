## Risk Assessment
The main risks associated with sourcing carbon fiber sheets from these suppliers include:
* Supply chain risks: Disruptions to the supply chain can impact lead times and pricing.
* Single-source risk: Relying on a single supplier can increase the risk of supply chain disruptions.
* Quality risks: Failure to meet quality certifications can impact the performance and safety of the final product.
To mitigate these risks, it is recommended to:
* Diversify the supply chain by sourcing from multiple suppliers.
* Develop a robust quality control process to ensure compliance with industry standards.
* Establish a strong relationship with the supplier to ensure communication and collaboration.
