## Risk Assessment
The main risks associated with the selected suppliers include:
* Supply chain risks: Disruptions to the supply chain can impact lead times and pricing.
* Single-source risk: Relying on a single supplier can increase the risk of supply chain disruptions and pricing fluctuations.
* Quality risks: Failure to meet quality standards can impact the performance and reliability of the lithium-ion battery components.
* Regulatory risks: Changes to regulations and standards can impact the suppliers' ability to comply with requirements.

To mitigate these risks, it is recommended to:
* Diversify the supply chain by working with multiple suppliers.
* Develop a robust quality control process to ensure compliance with standards and regulations.
* Monitor the suppliers' performance and adjust the supply chain as needed.
