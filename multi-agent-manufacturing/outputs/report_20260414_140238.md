## Risk Assessment
The main risks associated with sourcing carbon fiber sheets from these suppliers include:
* Supply chain risks: Disruptions to the supply chain, such as material shortages or transportation issues, could impact the availability of carbon fiber sheets.
* Single-source risk: Relying on a single supplier could pose a risk if they experience production issues or other disruptions.
* Quality risks: Variations in material quality could impact the performance and safety of the final product.
* Pricing risks: Fluctuations in material prices could impact the cost of the final product.

To mitigate these risks, it is recommended to:
* Diversify the supply chain by sourcing from multiple suppliers.
* Develop a robust quality control process to ensure material quality.
* Negotiate pricing agreements with suppliers to minimize price fluctuations.
* Develop a contingency plan to address potential supply chain disruptions.
